## 1. The Concept: "The VIP Supply Room"

We are transforming from just a supplier into a **complete business partner**.
The Public Website (`bcflameonline.com`) is our storefront window for attracting leads.
**This Portal** is the "Member's Club" for partners who have proven their loyalty (100+ days). It gives them the tools they need to sell *our* products to *their* customers more effectively.

---

## 2. The Problem We Are Solving

Our "Reseller" partners currently face three major bottlenecks that slow down our mutual sales:
1.  **Marketing Friction:** They lack high-quality photos to post on their social media.
2.  **Packaging Headaches:** They waste time and money hiring staff to pack and label products.
3.  **Inventory Blindness:** They have to message us to check if an item is in stock before they can sell it.

---

By taking the "hassle" out of reselling (packaging, photography, inventory checks), we make ourselves **essential**. We don't just sell them product; we sell them a *business in a box*.
